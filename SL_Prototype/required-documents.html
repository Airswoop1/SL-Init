REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
  • 1
    PHOTO ID
    Who are you and how old are you?
  • 2
    PROOF OF RESIDENCE
    Where do you live?
  • 3
    PROOF OF HOUSEHOLD COMPOSITION
    Who do you live with?
  • 4
    SOCIAL SECURITY #
    If you have one, what's your Social Security Number?
  • 5
    U.S. CITIZENSHIP
    Are you a US Citizen? Don't worry if you're not, you're still eligable for SNAP!
  • 6
    RENT EXPENSE
    How much do you pay for rent? A statement showing your shelter credit or housing voucher works!
  • 7
    UTILITY EXPENSES
    How much do you spend on gas, electric, water etc.? If nothing, don't worry about it.
  • 8
    PAY STUBS
    How much have you made recently (past 4 weeks)?
  • ANYTHING ELSE?
    Medical Bills, Dependent Care Costs, etc.
